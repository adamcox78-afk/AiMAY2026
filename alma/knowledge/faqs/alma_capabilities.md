# Alma Capabilities

## Voice Interaction
Alma can hear you speak and respond in a refined British accent via ElevenLabs.
Simply speak your command — Alma is always listening.

## Connected Integrations
- **Gmail**: Read, search, and send emails
- **Buffer**: Social media scheduling and analytics
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription revenue metrics
- **Meta**: Ad campaign management and insights
- **Browser**: Web research and automation via Chrome

## Sub-Agents
Alma coordinates specialized sub-agents:
1. **Marketing Agent** — Buffer + Meta campaigns
2. **Revenue Agent** — RevenueCat metrics
3. **Communications Agent** — Gmail management
4. **Research Agent** — Web search + browser

## Daily Brief
Every morning Alma delivers a spoken briefing covering:
- Email summary
- Revenue metrics
- Social media performance
- Campaign status
