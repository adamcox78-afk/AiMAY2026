The treatment

What your journey looks like

  1. 6–8 sessions

    Hair grows in cycles, so lasting smoothness usually takes six to eight sessions, spaced roughly 4–8 weeks apart.

  2. Quick visits

    An upper lip takes a couple of minutes; legs or a back are typically finished in under fifteen.

  3. Comfortable throughout

    The applicator glides while the tip cools — most people describe a warm-massage sensation rather than a snap.

  4. No downtime

    No recovery, no peeling. Head straight back to your day — just wear SPF on treated skin that sees the sun.
