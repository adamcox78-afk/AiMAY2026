How Alma TED Works: Sonophoresis

Alma TED uses acoustic sound waves and air pressure — a process called sonophoresis (TransEpidermal Delivery) — to temporarily open pathways in the scalp and drive a hair-nourishing serum down to the follicle. No needles. No micro-injuries. No downtime.

Sonophoresis is supported by decades of independent, peer-reviewed research on ultrasound-enhanced transdermal delivery — the same science Alma Lasers engineered into the TED applicator.
